Crescent Lane

A Novel by K. TingTing Gray

To the outside world, Mei, an investment banker in Chicago, is the embodiment of an immigrant's success story. Yet behind the façade of her contented American dream, she is plagued by shame and deeply suppressed secrets. Born during the Cultural Revolution in China, she survived the brutalities and betrayals of family, friends, and community. When an unexpected business assignment brings her back to China, she finds herself at a personal and ethical crossroads. At stake are either her own livelihood and future or those of thousands of Chinese farmers. In her search for answers, she discovers that to make the right choice and find redemption, she must first confront her past and reclaim all she had left behind, including a part of herself.
